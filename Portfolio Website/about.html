About Me

Hi! Let me tell you a little bit about myself. I've always been proactive about my passion for programming. I first discovered my passion while taking AP Computer Science A in 10th grade. Since then, my focus is learning. What I love about computer science is how interconnected the discipline is. I love the challenge of understanding the concepts I learned and combining them to problem solve. I love the idea of making a product that people worldwide could benefit from for years. Computer science is human-centered, and building computer software is an opportunity to serve others, which is how I want to apply my skills.

I enjoy playing my favorite video games, watching soccer, practicing chess, and honing my cooking skills when I'm not studying.
